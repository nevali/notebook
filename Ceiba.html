Ceiba

https://github.com/projectceiba/

  • Distributed & decentralised social network
  • Built upon:
    • DAT archives [decentralised, crypto-backed storage]
    • public-key cryptography [security, privacy]
    • Atom [activity feeds]
    • the Experience API (previously known as TinCan) [non-post activities within an Atom feed]
    • Turtle [social graph]
  • Strong bias towards ownership of identity and privacy.
  • Delivered primarily as a native app on mobile platforms, but public profiles browseable via both HTTPS and the DAT protocol
